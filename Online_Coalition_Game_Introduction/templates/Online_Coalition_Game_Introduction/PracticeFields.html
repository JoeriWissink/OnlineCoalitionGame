During the negotiation you will make offers using text boxes. On this page you will practice how to use these.

Below you find a text box. In such a text box you will type your offer. During bargaining you will make offers in increments of millions. Offers should also be made using integers, so without any decimals.

For example, if you want to offer 100 million, you will have to type the number 100, not the number 100,000,000.

Please practice this below by making an offer of 150 million.

When you have successfully entered an offer of 150 million you can continue to the next page.
